The Yesterweb Cafe is a 3D space on Mozilla Hubs where members of the Yesterweb can hang out!

How to use the Cafe

Commands

/fly
Make your avatar fly.
/grow
Increase the size of your avatar.
/shrink
Decrease the size of your avatar.

Controls

Place an object
Click the Place option at the bottom of the screen.
You can also place links as objects! This means you can place a website, a music stream, or a YouTube video.
Please be mindful of the fact that placing many objects will degrade the quality for others with less powerful PCs.
Manipulate an object
Holding down Spacebar while looking at an object will cause a menu will appear.
Pinning an object
Once you place an item, it stays as long as you are logged in. When you leave the room, your items will disappear. This can be avoided by pinning the object from the interaction menu, but please be aware that mods may remove your pinned items if the quality of the room starts to degrade.

Connection Tips

If you are experiencing slowness and lagging in the room, try some of the following tips:

  1. Change browsers; folks have had varying luck with Firefox and Chrome.
  2. Click on the three-dots menu labelled More at the bottom-right of the screen
    • Choose Preferences
    • Click on the Misc tab at the top
      • Change Material Quality to "Low"
      • Uncheck Enable Dynamic Shadows
      • Last resort: Change the Max Resolution to something smaller; things won't look as good, but they'll run much better!
    • Once making these changes, you'll be asked to leave the room and reload it.
